🛂 Visa & Immigration

Visa Application Guide by Type for Foreign Researchers in Korea (E-3, F-2, F-5)

✕
Visa & Immigration PDF Available Bilingual (KO/EN) 📅 Updated 2025.09.01
Overview

This guide covers the eligibility requirements, application procedures, and renewal/extension methods for each type of residence status available to foreign researchers conducting research activities in Korea.

Main Visa Types
  • E-3 (Research) — For foreigners conducting research at domestic research institutes or universities
  • F-2 (Residence) — Can be converted to residence status upon meeting specific criteria
  • F-5 (Permanent Residence) — Eligible to apply for permanent residency after a certain period of stay
  • D-8, D-9 — Applicable to researchers working in industry
Application Procedure
  • Prepare an official letter from your institution and employment contract
  • Visit the Immigration Office in person or apply online (Hi Korea)
  • Review period after submission (approx. 7–14 business days)
  • Receive your visa and Alien Registration Card (ARC)
Important Notes

Renewal applications must be completed at least 30 days before your stay period expires. Applying after expiry may result in a fine.
