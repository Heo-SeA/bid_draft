I signed my first jeonse (전세) lease in Korea — what an experience
🇩🇪
Anon_Researcher_582 Nov 3, 2025 · 14:22 Germany Materials Engineering
I finally completed a jeonse (전세 / lump-sum deposit lease) contract in Seoul after 3 months in Korea. The whole process — from finding a realtor to understanding the contract — was surprisingly complex.

I learned from this community about the importance of enrolling in the jeonse deposit insurance (전세보증보험) through HUG (Korea Housing Finance Corporation). It saved me from potential risk! For anyone going through the same thing, check the Settlement Info board — it's really helpful.
