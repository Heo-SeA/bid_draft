Step 1. Eligibility Check

Verify whether you are eligible to participate in national R&D projects and confirm your visa status requirements

Key Checkpoints
🛂 Visa status: Confirm you hold a research-eligible visa (E-3 Research, F-2 Residence, F-5 Permanent Residency, etc.)
🏛️ Verify that your affiliated institution qualifies as an eligible R&D participant (universities, research institutes, corporate R&D labs)
📋 Check whether foreign researchers are restricted from specific programs (some security-classified projects may be restricted)
📞 Inquire with your institution's Research Office or Industry-Academia Cooperation Foundation
⚠️ Important Notes
⚠️ Projects related to national security may restrict participation of foreign researchers
